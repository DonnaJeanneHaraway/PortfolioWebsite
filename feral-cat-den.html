Feral Cat Den

In the Summer of 2023 I worked as a intern for indie game studio Feral Cat Den. I worked on animations and concept art for their game Nirvana Noir. I also work on early concept art for a prospective game titled Filth.

Animation for Nirvana Noir

Animations for in game cut scenes and trailer for Nirvana Noir. All animated with vectors in Adobe Animate.

Tea Master Designs

Character design ideations for the Tea Master in Nirvana Noir.

No Man Concept Art

Sketches for developement of main character No Man in Nirvana Noir.

Filth Concept Art

Early concept art for the game Filth, which begins at a punk concert where a character falls into a toilet. The toilet becomes a portal to a desert world. The game would feature a talking dog that guides the main character through the desert, where they must solve puzzles to return to reality.
